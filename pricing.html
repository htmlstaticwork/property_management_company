Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any setup fees? ▼

No, we do not charge any setup or onboarding fees. You can create your account and start listing properties immediately after choosing a plan.

Can I switch plans later? ▼

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your dashboard. If you upgrade, the price will be prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle.

How do payments work? ▼

We accept all major credit cards. Payments are processed securely via our payment partner. You'll receive an automated invoice every billing cycle.

Do you provide legal lease templates? ▼

Yes, Professional and Enterprise plans include a library of state-specific, attorney-vetted lease agreements and legal notices.
